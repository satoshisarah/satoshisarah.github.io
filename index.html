BITCOIN
IS FOR
EVERYONE

Women and non-binary people are notoriously
underrepresented in the Bitcoin community.

We strive to bring these voices into the
conversation by creating a space that allows
us to connect, build, learn, teach, and engage
on an equal playing field.
